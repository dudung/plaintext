## root
+ A root $x = r_n$ of a function $f(x)$ is when it makes
$$\tag{1}
f(r_n) = 0.
$$
+ It can also be a root when
$$\tag{2}
g(r_n) = h(r_n),
$$
since
$$\tag{3}
f(x) = g(x) - h(x)
$$
can be defined.
